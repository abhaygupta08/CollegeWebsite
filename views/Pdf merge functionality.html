How Do You Merge PDF Files Explained

When you add multiple files together, you maintain the size and orientation of all the pages within the original files. Using our PDF combiner and PDF joiner does not prevent you from making alterations to the document, if required, such as changing the order of the pages of your PDF. Before or after merging two PDFs or more, you can use our Split tool to remove any pages within your original documents that you don’t need in the final PDF.
